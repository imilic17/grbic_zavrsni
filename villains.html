Villain Name: Toga

Real Name: Himiko Toga

Quirk: Transform

Friends: Twice, Dabi

Enemies: U.A. Students, Pro Heroes

Story

Toga is a member of the League of Villains. Her quirk allows her to transform into others by consuming their blood. She's obsessed with love and has a twisted sense of affection. Despite her violent tendencies, she's deeply loyal to those she cares about.

Tenko Shimura - Quirk: Decay

Villain Name: Shigaraki

Real Name: Tenko Shimura

Quirk: Decay

Friends: League of Villains

Enemies: All Might, U.A. Students

Story

Shigaraki is the leader of the League of Villains. With his Decay quirk, anything he touches with all fingers crumbles to dust. He aims to bring chaos and destroy hero society, continuing the legacy of his master, All For One.

All For One - Quirk: All For One

Villain Name: All For One

Real Name: Unknown

Quirk: All For One

Friends: Tomura Shigaraki

Story

All For One is the mysterious and powerful villain who can steal and transfer quirks. He was once the most feared threat in society and serves as a mentor to Shigaraki. Even after being heavily injured, he continues to influence the villain world from the shadows.
