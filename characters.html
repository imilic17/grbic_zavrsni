Class 1-A Students

Class 1-A is the spotlight class at U.A. High School. Its students have been put through some of the toughest challenges, making them strong future heroes. Characters like Izuku Midoriya, Katsuki Bakugo, Shoto Todoroki, and Ochaco Uraraka show rapid growth and adaptability. Their journey has been marked by fierce training, villain encounters, and intense competitions.

Class 1-B Students

Though often outshined by Class 1-A, Class 1-B boasts many powerful students with unique quirks and strategic minds. They are known for their unity, training discipline, and leadership under Vlad King. Students like Monoma and Kendo have stood out in multiple joint training battles, proving their capabilities as future pro heroes.
