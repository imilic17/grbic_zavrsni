Izuku Midoriya - Quirk: One For All

Hero Name: Deku

Real Name: Izuku Midoriya

Quirk: One For All

U.A. Class: 1-A

Friends: Uraraka, Iida, Todoroki

Enemies: Shigaraki, League of Villains

Story

Midoriya was born Quirkless in a world where 80% of the population has powers. Despite this, he always dreamed of becoming a hero like All Might. His determination paid off when All Might saw his potential and passed on the legendary Quirk "One For All" to him. Midoriya enrolled in U.A. High School and quickly proved himself through hard training, intelligence, and a strong sense of justice. He constantly pushes his limits, striving to protect others and live up to the legacy of All Might while forging deep bonds with his classmates and confronting powerful enemies.
